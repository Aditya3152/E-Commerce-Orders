# Executive Summary — E-Commerce Orders Analysis

**Objective:**  
Analyze E-Commerce sales data to identify top-performing products, key markets, and revenue trends for better business decision-making.

---

### Key Insights

1. **80/20 Product Effect:**  
   Roughly 20% of the products contribute to nearly 80% of total sales.  
   → *Focusing on these top SKUs can maximize revenue efficiency.*

2. **Seasonal Demand Peaks:**  
   Sales show clear spikes around holiday seasons, suggesting seasonal marketing and inventory planning opportunities.  
   → *Timing campaigns around these months can lift sales performance.*

3. **Geographical Concentration:**  
   The **United Kingdom** dominates total orders and revenue, followed by a few secondary markets.  
   → *Indicates potential for regional diversification or market expansion.*

4. **Order Value Distribution:**  
   Most transactions fall within a moderate price range, with outliers contributing significantly to revenue.  
   → *A tiered pricing or bundle strategy can capture both mid-range and high-value customers.*

---

### Business Implications

- Prioritize marketing and stock availability for **top-selling products**.  
- Plan **holiday season campaigns** ahead of demand peaks.  
- Explore **international market expansion** opportunities beyond the UK.  
- Implement **data-driven dashboards** to track product and regional performance continuously.
